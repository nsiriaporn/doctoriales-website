BoostUrCAreer

BoostUrCAreer project aims at implementing at Université Côte d’Azur and with the support from the European Commission and the Conseil Region Sud-Provence-Alpes-Côte d’Azur a multidisciplinary doctoral programme in e-health.

This project has received funding from EU's Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme under the Marie Sklodowska-Curie grant agreement N°847581

Doctoriales

As part of BoostUrCAreer programme, we are hosting an event each year to bring together PhD students and professionals from around the world. We want to create a space to share researches and findings, as well as network with like-minded international researchers.
